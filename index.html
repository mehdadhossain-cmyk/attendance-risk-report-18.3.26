Management summary

Out of the current cohort, students are newly identified as at risk this week and students were already in the risk category.

In terms of attendance movement, students have improved, have declined further, and have shown no change.

The data suggests that intervention efforts are contributing to improvement for a large proportion of students. However, continued focus is required for declining and unchanged cases, particularly those who remain difficult to engage.

Moving forward, priority will be placed on newly identified risk students, declining attendance cases, and students with no change despite intervention.

Campus insights
